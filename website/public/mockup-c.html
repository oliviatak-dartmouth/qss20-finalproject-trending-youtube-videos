Neutral titles get more views than emotional ones, even after controlling for category, channel size, and days on trending.

01: The Question

Does emotional content go viral? Depends where you look.

A well-known 2012 study found emotionally charged content spreads more. But YouTube is driven by an algorithm that rewards watch time, not shares. This project tests whether the same rule applies.

Prior research predicts

High arousal titles spread more

Studies on NYT articles and Twitter both found emotional content performs better.

VS
This study found

Neutral titles win on YouTube

The algorithm rewards watch time. Neutral titles may convert clicks to sustained viewing more reliably.
